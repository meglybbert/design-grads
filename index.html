Design Class of 2021

Class of 2021, you’ve made it! After a college experience complicated by a pandemic, you’ve reached a major milestone in your lives: completing a bachelor’s degree. Over the past few years, we’ve seen you grow, learn and overcome adversity. Many of you have experienced some pretty tough challenges—all while continuing to design, create, make and strengthen your skills. We’re proud of the people you’ve become and the work you’ve produced along the way. Cheers to each of you! Don't forget to reach out and congratulate your fellow graduates listed below. And, if you're not sick of hearing from us already, we've got some advice for you as you start the next chapter of your lives.

– EWU Department of Design Faculty

Messages from Your Instructors

If there is some type of design you are really interested in, don't wait for some client to ask you to do it. Just start making!

– Sonja Durr

If you are stuck, frustrated, or just not feeling motivated. Go for a walk and visit a local pizza shop and have a slice of pizza.

– Travis Masingale

Give them something unexpected.

– Sam Mills

  • Some things can only be learned through making mistakes and messing up. Don't let the fear of screwing up hold you back.
  • When in doubt, choose kindness.

– Meg Lybbert

  • Sometimes all it takes is showing up.
  • Don't forget to think about the journey, the process and investigate along the way.
  • Sketch, sketch, sketch.
  • Explore both the good and bad.

– Ginelle Hustrulid

  • Be patient.
  • Look and listen.
  • Design for community, equity, and inclusion as often as possible.
  • Keep in touch!

– Mindy Breen

  • Seek help. Everyone has received help. Seek people to help. You have more to offer than you realize.
  • Have awareness.
  • Read a book. Then read another.
  • Accept death. Then go live life. And stop looking at screens. Right now. Turn it off.
  • Come back to visit us at Catalyst.

– P. Colin Manikoth
